Smart Archie

What is Smart Archie?

Smart Archie is an internal AI tool for Cathay Financial Holdings to accelerate cloud migration. It was built to address two pain points: cloud migration demand outpaced a small architect team, and diagrams drawn independently across teams lacked consistency.

The tool collects requirements through structured surveys and conversational dialogue, then automatically generates cloud architecture diagrams mapped to AWS and GCP services to ensure every output aligns with Cathay's technical policies. Planning cycles are reduced from weeks of back-and-forth into a single working session.

The main workspace: architecture diagram on the left, AI chatbot panel on the right for iterative refinement.

Key Features

What Smart Archie Does

Two-Mode Entry: Guided Survey or Template Start

Smart Archie supports two different entry modes. For PMs and non-technical stakeholders, the tool provides a Guided Mode, with a 6-module survey (cloud platform, network, compute, database, storage, diagramming tool) that walks them through structured questions until requirements are complete. Experienced architects can skip the survey and enter through Fast Mode, picking from 8 pre-built templates for common cloud scenarios (modern web, database CDC, multi-cloud, and more).

Users choose their entry mode based on intent: guided survey for a fresh design, or fast-mode templates to adapt a familiar pattern to their context.

Fast Mode — 8 scenario templates.

Guided Mode — 6-module structured survey.

Automated Diagram Generation with Built-in Compliance

Manual drafting is slow, and independent architects produce inconsistent output that can miss internal policy requirements. Smart Archie maps validated requirements to AWS and GCP services and renders diagrams with Cathay's technical policies and templates embedded, so every output follows the same enterprise standards. Prompt and generation pipelines were optimized to cut token usage by 80–90% and bring end-to-end diagram generation down to about one minute.

Chatbot-Powered In-Place Editing

Even a good first draft rarely lands in one shot. After generation, users refine the diagram directly through the built-in chatbot: adding a security module, swapping a service, changing icons, or restructuring layers, all through natural language. Manual edits remain available at any point, so the workflow supports true human-in-the-loop collaboration rather than a one-shot generation.

Version Control & Change Tracking

Cross-team communication often kept only the final diagram. When a design decision was later questioned, the reasoning was lost. Smart Archie records every diagram update with a change log, letting stakeholders view the current design and trace how requirements evolved.
